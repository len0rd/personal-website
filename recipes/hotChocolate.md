# Hot Chocolate

#dessert #beverage

## Ingredients

Measure | Unit | Weight | Ingredient
--------|------|--------|-----------
1 | c | | Sugar
1 | c | | Water
1 | tsp | | Salt
4 | c | | Milk
4 | tbsp | | Cocoa

## Instructions

1. Mix sugar, salt, cocoa, and water in a blender. Heat to boiling.
2. Add milk. Heat but do NOT boil. Serve
